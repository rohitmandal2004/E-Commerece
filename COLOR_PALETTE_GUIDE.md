## Primary Color Palette

### Purple Shades (Primary)

- **Deep Purple**: `#6A1B9A` - Main brand color (Light mode)
- **Electric Purple**: `#7C4DFF` - Main brand color (Dark mode)
- **Light Purple**: `#B388FF` - Accents and highlights
- **Purple Container**: `#E1BEE7` - Backgrounds and cards

### Teal Shades (Secondary)

- **Vibrant Teal**: `#00BFA5` - Secondary actions, success states
- **Deep Teal**: `#00897B` - Hover states, gradients
- **Teal Container**: `#B2DFDB` - Light backgrounds

### Orange Shades (Tertiary)

- **Bright Orange**: `#FF6F00` - CTAs, highlights, discounts
- **Light Orange**: `#FFE0B2` - Backgrounds, cards

### Accent Colors

- **Hot Pink**: `#FF4081` - Special offers, beauty category
- **Gold**: `#FFD700` - Star ratings, premium elements
- **Amber**: `#FFC400` - Warnings, highlights
- **Bright Blue**: `#2979FF` - Electronics category

## Semantic Colors

### Success

## Background Colors

### Light Mode

- Background: `#FAFAFA` (Off-white)
- Surface: `#FFFFFF` (Pure white)
- Surface Variant: `#F5F5F5` (Light gray)

### Dark Mode

- Background: `#0F0F0F` (Deep black)
- Surface: `#1A1A1A` (Dark gray)
- Surface Variant: `#2A2A2A` (Medium gray)

## Text Colors

### Light Mode

- On Background: `#1A1A1A` (Near black)
- On Surface: `#1A1A1A` (Near black)
- On Primary: `#FFFFFF` (White)

### Dark Mode

- On Background: `#F0F0F0` (Off-white)
- On Surface: `#F0F0F0` (Off-white)
- On Primary: `#FFFFFF` (White)

## Usage Guidelines

### Price Display

- Original Price (strikethrough): Medium opacity text
- Discounted Price: Primary color (#6A1B9A or #7C4DFF)
- Rupee Symbol: Always use â‚¹ instead of $

### Ratings

- Stars: `#FFD700` (Gold)
- Rating count: Medium opacity text

### Buttons

- Primary CTA: Purple gradient
- Secondary: Teal solid
- Tertiary: Orange outline

### Cards

- Elevation: Use surface colors
- Border: Light outline (#E0E0E0 or transparent)
- Hover: Slight color shift or elevation increase

## Accessibility Notes

- All color combinations meet WCAG AA contrast requirements
- Dark mode colors are optimized for OLED displays
- High contrast between text and backgrounds
- Color is never the only way to convey information

## Design Philosophy

Our color palette reflects:

- **Luxury & Premium**: Deep purples and golds
- **Energy & Vibrancy**: Bright teals and oranges
- **Modern & Fresh**: Clean backgrounds, bold accents
- **Indian Market Appeal**: Warm colors, vibrant palette
- **Trust & Reliability**: Professional color balance
